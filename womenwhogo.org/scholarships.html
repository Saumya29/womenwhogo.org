GopherCon US 2018

To qualify you must identify as a woman or non-binary. Applications are due by June 22nd and we plan to notify recipients by the week of July 2nd. We will let everyone know when the selection process is complete so that you can plan accordingly.

Our goal is to send as many people as possible to GopherCon. Each scholarship is different, depending on what you need help with:

  • GopherCon ticket
  • Plane tickets
  • Hotel/Airbnb
  • Food, taxi, etc.

If your employer can pay for any part of your expenses, we ask that you take advantage of that first and use the scholarship for only uncovered expenses.
